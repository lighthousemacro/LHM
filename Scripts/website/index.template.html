THE DIAGNOSTIC DOZEN

Twelve macro pillars, organized into three engines. Each captures a distinct dimension of the cycle. Together they feed a layered risk architecture that combines quantitative rigor with experienced judgment.

// 017 Pillars

Macro Dynamics

The real economy. Labor flows, price composition, growth momentum, and the four end-markets that move underneath the cycle.

Monetary Mechanics

The plumbing. Treasury fiscal dynamics, credit and financial conditions, and the Fed liquidity regime that sits upstream of every risk asset.

  • Government · fiscal dynamics, debt sustainability

Market Structure

Price-action internals and positioning. Breadth, leadership, divergence, sentiment, and the tape's read on what is consensus versus variant.

  • Market Structure · breadth, momentum, participation

// 02The Method

THE PHILOSOPHY

// 01The Core

Framework over Forecast

The map is the value. The forecast is downstream. Twelve pillars, three engines, one map. We publish the lens, not the lottery ticket.

// 02Principle

Process over Outcome

Outcomes are noisy and luck-laden. We grade ourselves on whether the framework was applied, not on whether the print agreed.

// 03Principle

Strong Views, Weakly Held

Every view ships with the conditions that would break it. Explicit invalidation criteria, published in advance, never retro-fitted.

// 04Principle

One Book, Two Ways

A macro framework read paired with a technical read of price. Macro and market can diverge. Same book, one consistent risk posture.

// 08The Author

BOB SHEEHAN

"The Lighthouse" was the name of Bob's house in college, where he first began studying finance and economics. Over the years it evolved into a research firm built on quantitative rigor and systematic macro analysis, serving portfolio managers, advisory firms, and business leaders who need to understand what is actually happening beneath the headlines.

Bob's neurodivergent wiring is core to the firm's edge. Most analysts specialize in one domain. His ADHD-driven intellectual restlessness pushes him across the full macro landscape, from Fed plumbing to labor economics to credit markets to Treasury microstructure to crypto integration and quantitative modeling. The result is depth across domains, and the ability to see connections specialists miss by staying in silos.
